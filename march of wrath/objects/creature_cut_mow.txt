creature_cut_mow

This file removes a lot of the zaniness of DF. It also limits the animals encountered to the "occident" and "near orient". If it would have been in a Roman arena, I intend to leave it in. The ranges of these animals are considered from ancient times, not their current and regretably diminished habitats.

Remove this file or any individual cuts if you want these creatures back in your game. 

Animal-men and giant versions are culled with their base creature. Animal-men and giants for the unculled species will be culled in the monster_cut_mow file.
